### **# Introduction**



"Medi-Lens is designed to reduce confusion around medical documents to bridge 

the gap between complex medical jargon and patient understanding".



Core Capabilities: \* Upload images of handwritten or digital prescriptions.



Analyze complex lab reports for easier interpretation.



Gain insights into medicine usage and dosages.



Detect potential risks or safety warnings based on extracted data.



**Objective:** To help users understand medical prescriptions and lab reports through advanced image analysis.



**How it works:** This documentation provides a comprehensive guide on the system architecture, local installation, and the AI pipeline used for health data extraction.
