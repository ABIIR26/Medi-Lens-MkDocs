\## üöÄ How It Works

Medi-Lens follows a 4-step intelligence pipeline to ensure data integrity and clinical relevance.







1\.  \*\*Ingestion:\*\* The user uploads a photo of a prescription or lab report via the Next.js interface.

2\.  \*\*Transformation:\*\* Our Vision Transformer (ViT) model removes noise and digitizes handwritten text.

3\.  \*\*Analysis:\*\* The Medical NLP engine maps text to valid drug names (NDC) and lab benchmarks.

4\.  \*\*Insight:\*\* Users receive a simplified summary, safety alerts, and a digital history.
